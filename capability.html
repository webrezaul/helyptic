Routing & Prototyping



PCBs (printed circuit boards) are the backbone of electronics and a faulty PCB means a waste of money and time

PCB design requires knowledge about electronic components, current and voltage characteristics, Routing and soldering skills.

Helyptic will design and implement your PCB prototype using the most advanced routing and soldering Techniques.

Electronic Card Manufacturing

RFID has a wide range for usage in applications like Inventory management, Asset tracking, Personnel tracking, Controlling access to restricted areas, ID Badging, etc.
