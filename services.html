Hardware Development

PCB Design and Fabrication

Not only PCB assembly , but also a complete solution including mechanical parts assembly. We control the entire process from procurement of mechanical parts to meet any customers expectations in terms of quality and reliability.

3D Printing

  • Our powerful 3D printing technology produces highly accurate and durable parts capable of being used directly in end-use.
